voxel-mipmap-demo
=================
Demonstration of texture mapping with greedy meshing.

* Left click rotates
* Right click/shift pans
* Middle click/scroll/alt zooms

[Check it out in your browser](http://mikolalysenko.github.io/voxel-mipmap-demo/)
